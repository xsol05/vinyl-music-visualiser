# Vinyl Music Visualiser

🎵 **Visualise your music here!** https://magdelinehuang.com/vinyl-music-visualiser

---

I vibe coded this vinyl music visualiser app to visualise any music you want!

Just upload an audio file, move the needle onto the vinyl, and watch your music come to life. 

To pause / stop, simply move the needle away–just like a real vinyl!
